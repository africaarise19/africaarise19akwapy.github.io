Who We Are?

Akwappaddys is an African diaspora led non profit development NGO registered in Ireland and stand to empower the vulnerable young girls, and elderly women who have poor health as a result of poverty, hunger, and climate change through access to affordable resources and opportunities such as information, soft Loans, education, healthcare,

Areas of Work

African in Diaspora led Humanitarian and development Intervention through collaboration and partnerships linking Irish-Uganda

- Day Care Centre for Elderly , young mother in Pallisa,
- Support service for elderly and girl child, young and single mothers in Pallisa
- Short term volunteering opportunities for students, proffessionals and retired senior citizens through cultural exchange visits
- We select and engage good will ambassadors and celebrate success through award giving ceremony
- we participate in integration and social inclusion events in Ireland and in Africa
- To promote spirit of social enteprneurship to raise the much needed funds
